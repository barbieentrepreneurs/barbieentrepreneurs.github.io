Visimp Helper

The Visimp Helper was the project my team and I worked on for our Capstone project. Out project won an honorable mention at the annual UOIT design exhibition. It is a smartphone application targeted to those who are visiually impaired and it is meant to improve their day-to-day lives by offering them services such as object identification, reading text, directions, amongst other features which are highlighted in the video.
